About the Game

Neon Drift allows players to pilot high-tech hover vehicles through challenging neon tracks.
You'll need to nail those drifts, dodge obstacles, and manage your time if you want to beat each level. Each track introduces new hazards, ramps, and drifting opportunities to keep gameplay fresh.
The goal is to get really good at controlling your ride, go as fast as possible, and look cool doing it.

Core Mechanics

Navigate neon tracks at high speed

Perform precision drifts for time bonuses

Avoid obstacles and falling off edges

Collect boost pads to increase velocity

Unlock new vehicles and track variations

Later tracks introduce moving obstacles, jumps, and split routes.
